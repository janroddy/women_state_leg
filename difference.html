Gains in Female Representation in State Legislatures from 2018 Midterm Election

Hypothesis:The 2018 Midterm election resulted in increases in percentage of women in state legislatures due to high voter turnout and record numbers of women running for office.

There has been a lot of attention to the increase in Congressional gains in gender parity as a result of the 2018 Midterm election, but there has also been major gains in the state legislatures.

In 2014 2,521 woman ran for state legislature.

In 2016 2,649 woman ran for state legislature.

In 2018 3,418 woman ran for state legislature.
